Light Pollution

Dim the lights, save the night!

About My Topic

Light pollution is the overuse of artificial light at night, which can disrupt the natural patterns of wildlife, human sleep, and the night sky. It can also have negative health effects on humans by increasing the risk of dieases.

Causes of light pollution:

  • Glare
  • Light Trespass
  • Skyglow
